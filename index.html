The Leafy Greens

Indie Rock band and vegetable enthusiasts

Who are we?

The Leafy Greens are an indie rock band who eat their vegetables. They were founded in August of 2018 by Ari Perez, Jonas Bakelaar and Michael Vemvakas. Currently the band's members include Ari Perez, Jonas Bakelaar, Michael Vemvakas, and Saatvik Bhayana.

To date, they have released one single called Cut You Some Slack, and is available everywhere you stream music. They continue to work on original music, and will be releasing an album in the coming months.
